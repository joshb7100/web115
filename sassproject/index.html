My Favorite Dogs

I love almost all kinds of dogs! But there are three main kinds that I prefer the most. These three kinds are:

My Inspiration

One of the reasons I love dogs so much is that for the majority of my life I have had a dog. My family had a golden retriever when I was born who lived to be 13 years old and we currently have another golden retriever, Comet, who is currently nine yet still acts like a puppy!
