German Shepards

My other favorite type of dog is the german shepard. Similar to huskies, I always felt they looked strong and regal and they seemed like they would be very loyal companions.

Very Intelligent Partners

The reason this breed always seemed loyal to me is through their excessive use in the police force and army. I believe that german shepards are supposed to be considered one of the smartest dog breeds, which makes sense if they are being chosen to help with such important professions.
